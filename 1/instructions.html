Task Overview

We are creating product review templates highlighting important features of different products (e.g. noise is an important feature of an oscillating fan). Your task is to identify and describe the two most important non-photography related features people should consider when buying a recreational drone.

Use Our Search Engine

In this task, please use our search engine on the top of the screen to help you figure out which two features are the most important. Please do not use any other search engines or open up new browser windows.

Write Two Feature Descriptions

Use the writing description box on the right side of the screen to create your two feature descriptions based on your searches. For each feature you select, write one sentence describing the feature, and one sentence explaining why it's important. When you are done creating your feature descriptions, click the button at the bottom of the screen.

Evaluation

We will be selecting some of the feature descriptions from these HITs to include in our product review template. Other Turkers will be rating your descriptions based on 1) how important the features are to someone looking to buy this product and 2) how easy it is to understand your descriptions of the features. Each description rated in the top 50% of submissions will receive a bonus payment.

Finishing the Task

Click "Submit Descriptions" to send your descriptions on to other Turkers for evaluation.

After you submit your work, take a short survey to finish the task.

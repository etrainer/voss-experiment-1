Study Consent Form

This task is part of a research study conducted by Laura Dabbish and James Herbsleb at Carnegie Mellon University.

The purpose of the research is to understand online work environments.

Procedures

You will take part in a collaborative writing task. You will be expected to contribute content to a wiki, summarize or edit existing content. The amount of time you spend beyond the minimum contribution is up to you, but you may be rewarded more for greater contributions or if your writing reaches a high level of quality.

You will be asked to complete questionnaire items about your experiences during the task.

Participant Requirements

Participation in this study is limited to individuals age 18 and older.

Risks

The risks and discomfort associated with participation in this study are no greater than those ordinarily encountered in daily life or during other online activities.

Benefits

There may be no personal benefit from your participation in the study but the knowledge received may be of value to humanity.

Compensation & Costs

You will be paid for participation. The amount of your reward may be based on your performance and the quality of the work you do. There will be no cost to you if you participate in this study.

Confidentiality

The data captured for the research includes your name and email address only if you are willing to provide it.

Voluntary Participation

Your participation in this research is voluntary. You may discontinue participation at any time during the research activity. If you have any questions about the research you should contact Laura Dabbish at dabbish@cs.cmu.edu.
